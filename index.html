Frequently asked questions

Q1: What should I look for when buying sunglasses?
When buying sunglasses, the most important thing to look for is a sticker or tag that indicates that the glasses will block UV rays. These are the light rays that can damage your eyes, causing cataracts, macular degeneration, photokeratitis (sunburn of the eye), and wrinkles.
Q2: How can I know what my prescription is?
You can find your prescription by visiting an eye care professional or optometrist. If you already have a prescription, simply enter it during checkout. If you don’t have a prescription, we recommend booking an eye exam with a local optometrist.
Q3: How do I choose the right frame for my face shape?
Choosing the right frame depends on your face shape. You can also try on frames virtually using our virtual try-on feature.
Q4: What materials are your frames made from?
We offer frames made from a variety of materials, including acetate, stainless steel, titanium, and eco-friendly options. Each material has its own unique qualities acetate is lightweight and customizable, stainless steel is durable, and titanium is both strong and lightweight.
Q5: Do you offer prescription lenses for sunglasses?
Yes, we offer prescription lenses for sunglasses. Simply choose the frames you want, and we’ll add prescription lenses tailored to your vision needs.
Q6: How long will it take to receive my glasses?
Standard processing time for prescription eyewear is usually between 7-14 business days. Shipping time varies depending on your location and shipping method. We offer expedited shipping options if you need your glasses sooner.
Q7: How do I clean and care for my glasses?
We offer frames made from a variety of materials, including acetate, stainless steel, titanium, and eco-friendly options. Each material has its own unique qualities acetate is lightweight and customizable, stainless steel is durable, and titanium is both strong and lightweight.
Q8: Can I add coatings or enhancements to my lenses?
Yes! We offer a variety of lens coatings and enhancements, including anti-reflective coatings, scratch-resistant coatings, blue light filters, and UV protection. You can add these options during the checkout process.
