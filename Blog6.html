6 Easy Eye Exercises for Focus, Eye Strain & More | Eye Health

No, it's not your legs. It's your eyes1,2. In simple terms, six eye muscles are constantly moving to readjust your eyeball so you can see. In just one hour of reading, the eye makes almost 10,000 coordinated movements2. So, it's no surprise they need some rest. And just like every other muscle in the body, it’s important to keep the eye muscles strong. While you can’t treat myopia or hyperopia with eye exercises, they may help reduce eye strain and fatigue, which do affect the quality of your vision.
